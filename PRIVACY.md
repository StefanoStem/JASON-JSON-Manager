# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

JASON does not collect, transmit, or share any data. All JSON snippets and tab data are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local` and never leave your device.

- **No analytics or tracking**
- **No network requests**
- **No third-party services**

### Permissions used

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Save your snippets and tab names locally between sessions |
| `sidePanel` / `sidebar` | Display the editor in the browser sidebar |
